Federal Contracts
Search the FPDS site for contractors in Denmark: https://www.fpds.gov/fpdsng_cms/index.php/en/
-Search by Vendor Country
VENDOR_ADDRESS_COUNTRY_NAME:"Denmark"
-Search by Place of Performance (Note, the country must be in all capital letters here to work):
POP_COUNTRY_NAME:"DENMARK"
-You can narrow your search by any field in the federal contracts data. Data is exportable to CSV.
U.S. Food and Drug Administration import refusal data – OASIS
Find products that have been refused for entry into the U.S. from your country. Search monthly reports
by product or country. Download CSV files and search for your country’s ISO code. Denmark, for
example, would be “DK”. http://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/importrefusals/
