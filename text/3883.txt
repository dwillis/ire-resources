Gross

(GDP)	  
Personal	  
Income	  

Meaning	  
The	  total	  value	  of	  goods	  and	  services	  produced	  
within	  an	  area	  (a	  nation,	  a	  state,	  or	  a	  
metropolitan	  area)	  in	  a	  period.	  

Measures	  the	  income	  that	  is	  received	  by	  all	  
people	  from	  all	  sources.	  It	  includes	  wages	  and	  
salaries,	  supplements	  (like	  health	  insurance),	  
proprietors'	  income,	  property	  income	  (rent,	  
interest,	  and	  dividends),	  and	  personal	  current	  
transfer	  receipts,	  such	  as	  Social	  Security,	  less	  
contributions	  for	  government	  social	  insurance.	  	  
	  
The	  personal	  income	  of	  an	  area	  is	  the	  income	  
that	  is	  received	  by	  all	  the	  individuals	  who	  live	  in	  
the	  area.	  Thus,	  the	  estimates	  of	  personal	  income	  
are	  presented	  by	  the	  place	  of	  residence	  of	  the	  
income	  recipients	  –	  not	  by	  place	  of	  work.	  	  
Proprietors’	   Income	  of	  sole	  proprietorships	  (an	  
income	  
unincorporated	  business	  owned	  by	  a	  single	  
person)	  and	  partnerships	  (an	  unincorporated	  
business	  owned	  jointly	  by	  several	  people).

Per	  capita	  

Meaning	  
Generally	  excludes	  interest,	  dividends,	  and	  rent.	  
Payments	  to	  people	  for	  which	  no	  current	  
services	  are	  performed.	  Government	  payments	  
to	  individuals	  include:	  retirement	  and	  disability	  
insurance	  benefits,	  such	  as	  Social	  Security;	  
medical	  benefits,	  such	  as	  Medicare	  and	  
Medicaid;	  income	  maintenance	  benefits;	  	  
unemployment	  insurance	  compensation;	  
veterans	  benefits;	  and	  federal	  education	  and	  
training	  assistance.	  Payments	  from	  private	  
companies	  consist	  primarily	  of	  liability	  payments	  
for	  personal	  injury.	  
Per	  person.	  Per	  capita	  personal	  income	  is	  total	  
personal	  income	  divided	  by	  the	  population.
