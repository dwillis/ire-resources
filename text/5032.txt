Public Records Available in Michigan

Free public records available online
Prisoner locator: michigan.gov/otis
Federal prisoner locator: www.bop.gov/inmates
Sex offender registry: https://www.mipsor.state.mi.us/
Corporation lookup: http://www.dleg.state.mi.us/bcs_corp/sr_corp.asp
Verify a license: http://www.dleg.state.mi.us/bcs_corp/sr_corp.asp (Includes adult foster care homes;
asbestos contractors and abatement locations; child care homes and centers; children and adult foster
care camps; securities and commercial licensing; construction code licenses; hospitals, hopices, surgery
centers, nursing homes and substance abuse; health professional licenses; liquor licenses; occupational
licenses.
Legislation and laws: www.michiganlegislature.org
House Fiscal Agency: http://www.house.mi.gov/hfa/home.asp
Senate Fiscal Agency: http://www.senate.michigan.gov/sfa/
State auditor general: http://audgen.michigan.gov/
State employee contact search: http://www.state.mi.us/dit/default.aspx
Teacher search: https://mdoe.state.mi.us/MOECS/PublicCredentialSearch.aspx
Grocery store inspection search: https://secure1.state.mi.us/misafe/Default.aspx
Restaurant inspection search: http://swordsolutions.com/Inspections
Campaign finance disclosure: http://www.michigan.gov/sos/0,4670,7-127-1633_8723---,00.html
Lobbyist disclosure: http://www.michigan.gov/sos/0,4670,7-127-1633_11945---,00.html
Lawyer lookup: https://www.zeekbeek.com/SBM
Disciplined attorneys: http://www.adbmich.org/CHECKER.HTM
Disciplined judges: http://jtc.courts.mi.gov/
