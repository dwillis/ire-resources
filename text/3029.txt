Broadcast and CAR: Quick hits and visuals

Quick Hits
Fire! Crash! Death & Destruction! Its 3pm and your news director wants you to
“investigate.” Here’s what you say back:
I can get this for you in the next hour, if…
A plane crashes
Aircraft Registry Information
http://registry.faa.gov/aircraftinquiry/
Find the make, model, owner, city and state where the plane is registered
Aircraft Safety Information
http://www.ntsb.gov/ntsb/query.asp
Find how often accidents happen at that same location/airplane
A factory catches on fire or a worker is crushed
OSHA Inspection
http://www.osha.gov/oshstats/index.html
Find how many accidents have happened before, including how often the
factory/company has been issued serious violations, what the cause was
and how much they were fined.
A tanker just blew up and spewed gas/oil/chemicals all over the highway
Hazardous Materials Database at http://www.ire.org/datalibrary/
Find how often the company/stretch of road/chemical has been involved in
accidents
A kid just shot/stabbed someone at a school
State Disciplinary Database
Find how many kids have been expelled for carrying weapons in that
school/district
Someone just fell off a boat and drowned
Boating Accident Database at http://www.ire.org/datalibrary/
Find how often people drown in that same area, if the same kind of boat is
involved

I can get this to you by tomorrow’s newscast, if…
A bridge or a dam just fell down
The bridge and dam databases are available at
http://www.ire.org/datalibrary/
Use it to find how many structurally deficient bridges are in your area and
to pinpoint some really ugly ones.
A college student was just murdered/raped/etc.
Campus Crime database at http://www.ire.org/datalibrary/
Use it to find how many students have been murdered/raped/etc in the last
few years
A bank was just robbed or someone was assaulted
County Police/Sheriff’s Department’s UCR database
Use it to see how often banks (as well as pizza delivery men, etc) have
been robbed, assaulted, shot at, etc.
Um…that is a big time investigation…I can get that to you in a week or two
Foreclosures and Mortgage Data
HMDA at http://www.ire.org/datalibrary/

And when in doubt…go to this website:
http://www.ire.org/membership/listserv.html
Ask the NICAR list-serve what they would suggest. You will get an answer in 5 minutes
