LAW ENFORCEMENT
Courts
The courts are a treasure trove of documents and sources. They can provide
information about bankruptcy, divorce, criminal records and more.
A comprehensive directory of court records available through the Web:
http://www.courtreference.com
To search for cases involving a particular person in a criminal case in New York:
https://iapps.courts.state.ny.us/webcrim_attorney/Login
To search the system for a civil case:
https://iapps.courts.state.ny.us/nyscef/Login
For free access to many court documents, try:
http://dockets.justia.com/
Prison inmates
Prison records are readily available online. They will give you information on how
to contact an inmate.
Federal inmate locator: https://www.bop.gov/inmateloc/
State inmate locator: http://www.doccs.ny.gov/inmateinfo.html
VineLink offender databases: http://www.vinelink.com/index/isp
Sex offenders are on National Sex Offender Registry

Police
Press releases from the department are a steady source of breaking news. Get on
their email lists.

POLITICS
Politicians need contributions to run political campaigns. By law, they are
required to file who contributed to their campaign and what they spent their
money on.
NYS campaign records:
http://www.elections.ny.gov/CFViewReports.html
Federal campaign records:
www.fed.gov/finance/disclosure
www.opensecrets.org
Elected officials’ finances:
Financial disclosure forms are filed with the Ethics Commission of the agency or
municipality that employs the official.
www.followthemoney.org
Legislation and speeches:
http://thomas.loc.gov
Lobbyists:
http://www.opensecrets.org/lobby/search/plp

REAL ESTATE
Real estate records reveal who bought and sold property and for what price. They
will also show you whether the buyer took out a mortgage and from whom.

Property sales in New York State:
http://swcf.orpts.ny.gov/cfapps/salesWebProd/salesWeb/index.cfm
To pull deeds and mortgages, go to the clerk of the county where the property is
located.
Suffolk County Clerk (located in Riverhead):
http://www.suffolkcountyny.gov/Departments/CountyClerk/OnlineRecords.aspx
Nassau County Clerk (located in Mineola):
https://i2f.uslandrecords.com/NY/Nassau/D/Default.aspx?AspxAutoDetectCookie
Support=1
Land records for any county in NYS:
https://www.nylandrecords.com/nylr/NylrApp/index.jsp
Property values:
http://www.zillow.com/
www.realtor.com

PUBLIC MONEY
Public pay and benefits are a continuing source of interest. Payrolls also tell you
whether some has left employment.
Public payrolls, pensions and contracts in New York:
http://www.seethroughny.net/
http://www.openbooknewyork.com/

BUSINESS SEARCHES
Businesses must file incorporation records with the state. Public companies filed
with the Securities and Exchange Commission.
Securities and Exchange Commission:
https://www.sec.gov/edgar/searchedgar/companysearch.html
Department of State incorporation records:
http://www.dos.ny.gov/corps/bus_entity_search.html
Nexis Dun & Bradstreet reports.
(Note: They are good on some facts, but notoriously inaccurate when it comes to
sales.)
For business contacts and employees or ex-employees of a company:
www.linkedin.com
