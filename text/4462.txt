Where to look:
• Financial data: SEC Edgar, S&P Compustat, Bloomberg
• Tax liens, Bankruptcies, Lawsuits: Lexis/Nexis, PACER
• Health and safety data: State/Local, OSHA, Labor Dept.

• Licenses, Permits: City/County/State
• Regulators: FAA, DOTs, EPA, DEPs
• Market Research: Nielsen, IBIS, Hoovers, Gartner
• Universities: Researchers, Libraries
• Industry/Trade Associations
• Local => County => State => Federal
