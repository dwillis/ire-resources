The “Invisible Web”
Ø The Visible Web (“Surface Web”) is made up of
the typical results in search engines.
Ø The Invisible Web (“Deep Web”) includes
searchable databases / dynamic content.
Ø Some estimate the Invisible Web is 550 times
bigger than the Visible Web.
Ø Google says more than 1,000 federal
government sites can’t be “crawled.”
