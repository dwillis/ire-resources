A few of my favorite things :
The Internet Archive (“The Wayback Machine”): http://www.archive.org
American Factfinder (Census): http://factfinder.census.gov
Census Bureau International Database: http://www.census.gov/ipc/www/idbnew.html
World Bank data & statistics: http://www.worldbank.org/data/
Finding domain owners: http://www.allwhois.com/
Find a lawyer’s phone number: www.martindale.com. Or: Try the state’s bar association
website
Find places and make customized maps: www.nationalatlas.gov/
Find the distance between two places: www.indo.com/distance
Check on Congress: http://thomas.loc.gov/
