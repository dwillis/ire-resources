GENERAL RESOURCES/RECURSOS GENERALES
Google Scholar

http://scholar.google.com/

Google Scholar (beta) enables the search for scholarly literature, including peerreviewed papers, theses, books, preprints, abstracts and technical reports from all
broad areas of research. Use Google Scholar to find articles from a wide variety of
academic publishers, professional societies, preprint repositories and universities,
as well as scholarly articles available across the web. Information on books is
provided with links to libraries that have the book and to online booksellers. Some
articles are freely available—usually those from a non-profit organization or
government agency; articles published in academic journals may be available for
purchase from an online document delivery service.
Este nuevo servicio de Google permite la búsqueda para literatura académica y
científica de investigación, publicada en libros, artículos, informes técnicos, etc.
Provee acceso a trabajos disponibles en linea en el web “abierto” (gratuito) y
tambien de servicios de editoriales y entrega de documentos comerciales.
Incluye información donde se puede localizar un libro, que sea en una biblioteca
o desde una libreria en linea.
Google Alerts
http://www.google.com/alerts
http://www.google.com/alerts/faq.html?hl=en
Google Alerts are emails automatically sent to you when there are new Google
results for your search terms. We currently offer three types of alerts: 'News,'
'Web,' and 'News & Web.' This is a way to follow news stories and receive updates
whenever a new article appears.
“Google alerts” son mensajes de correo electrónico que avisan cuando nueva
información/artículos han sido publicado que pertenecen a los temas o a los
terminos que describe el tema en que esta interesado. Es una manera facil de
seguir eventos de interes y recibir las noticias al momento.

los países en desarrollo y particularmente de América Latina y el Caribe.

Handbook of Latin American Studies (HLAS),
http://lcweb2.loc.gov/hlas/
Citations and abstracts to social sciences and humanities literature on Latin
America from 1935 – present. HLAS is a selective, annual bibliography,
compiled by experts in many academic fields and is one of the best ways to
find important resources for Latin American studies. The HLAS includes
citations to books, journal articles, dissertations and online resources. When
available, links are provided to online, full text sources.
Citas bibliográficas y resúmenes de la literatura de ciencias sociales y

textos en linea disponibles.
Library of Congress—American Memory
http://memory.loc.gov/ammem/
American Memory provides free and open access through the Internet to
written and spoken words, sound recordings, still and moving images, prints,
maps, and sheet music that document the American experience. Focus is
mainly the history of the United States, but a search on the keywords
“Mexico and border” provides access to more than 500 primary historical
documents, photographs, maps, recordings, etc.
El proyecto “La Memoria Americana” provee acceso abierto a documentos,
grabaciones, imagenes fotográficos y video, mapas, música, etc. de la
historia documental de los Estados Unidos. Una busqueda de “Mexico and
border” (por ejemplo) provee mas de 500 recursos báscios para la
investigación, como mapas, fotos, etc.
