“In my role as Chair of the United States Conference of
Mayors Immigration Task Force and as the Mayor of
Laredo, I have pushed for a permanent, legislative
solution to our ineffective and unjust immigration
system” Mayor Raul G. Salinas. Laredo, Texas

Facts
• More than 47 percent of United States
international trade headed for Mexico and more
than 36 percent of Mexican international trade
crosses through the Laredo port of entry.

Enhanced border security

initiatives:

• Securing the border would serve as a prerequisite to
the path to citizenship for undocumented immigrants
• Earned pathway to citizenship for undocumented
immigrants who meet eligibility requirements
• Major reforms to legal immigration and family and
employment visa programs
• Allocation of over $8 billion in fees and penalties
generated from the bill to deficit reduction
• The fees and penalties would be paid by undocumented
immigrants as they move through the path to
citizenship

A call to Laredoans
• “I encourage all Laredoans to contact our Texas federal
representatives, as well as the Senate “Gang of Eight” to let
them know that immigration reform is right for America and
for all Americans, not just the 11 million undocumented
immigrants who have been contributing to this nation,
through their hard work, education, the economy and
service in our armed forces”
Mayor Raul G. Salinas

COLEF
• En el 2do. trimestre de 2012, la Emif Norte captó
192,500 desplazamientos migratorios laborales que
arribaron a las 12 principales ciudades de la frontera
norte.
• El 59% de estos desplazamientos tenían por destino
una de estas ciudades y el 41% tenían el propósito de
cruzar a Estados Unidos.
• De este flujo migratorio internacional, el 18% eran
mujeres.
