Helpful terms and resources
Term definitions
● geocoding​ – the process of transforming an address into a point on the Earth’s surface
● latitude/longitude​ - coordinates to find a point on the Earth’s surface
● types of geographic data
○ points​ – dots on a map (restaurants, schools, bus stops, etc.)
○ lines​ – series of connected points (streets, GPX traces, etc.)
○ polygons/shapes​ - areas (country borders, census blocks, etc.)
