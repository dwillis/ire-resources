Some general guidelines
●
●
●
●
●

Always verify information found online with primary source documents
Be prepared to sift through a lot of hay to find your needles
There is no “magic” website
People aren’t that creative
Extensive backgrounding is key for one-shot interviews

Some general guidelines
●
●
●
●

Solid backgrounding can lead to a story
Have a clear, focused research strategy
Back up online sleuthing with shoe-leather reporting -- verify and extend
anything you find online
“Is there anything in your background I need to know about?”

PDFs are not data!
●
●

Check the document properties
Check the law: In many states, you have the right to request data as data, if it
exists in that format

“Each agency shall provide a copy of an electronic record in the format requested
if the requested format is one that has been used by the agency to create copies
for its own use or for provision to other agencies.”
-- California Government Code 6253.9(a)2
