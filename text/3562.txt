` Building codes
` Warnings
` Local shelter retrofit plans and preparations
` Mitigation plans – now required by Federal &
State Law
` Shelter and evacuation plans including roads
to use, who to evacuate, elderly, weak &
infirmed, animals
` Worst case scenario-What’s the Plan?
