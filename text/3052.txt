Search methods
Advanced searching
Truncating names or using wildcards or proximity
Reading about that search engine/Web site
Know what is in the database (not all counties) (if you don't get a hit, doesn't
mean it isn't there or doesn't exist)
Data entry errors/names spelled wrong/McGraw vs Mc Graw/Commas or no
commas
Use primary sources
