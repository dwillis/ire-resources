DATA: YOUR BEST SOURCE
´ When starting a beat or a project, get to know

the data, along with the people
´ Ask the humans:
« What do people in the field read?
« How do they measure success/failure?
« What are key ratios/analytics?
« What are important data sources?
« Who are the analysts they respect?
