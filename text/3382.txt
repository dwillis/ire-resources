Getting bank data
 Each bank reports quarterly to FDIC (“call report”

or “condition statement”)
 Reports are public
 Highly detailed (a 50-page PDF)
 This is clean data

Tracking one bank
 Go to FDIC home page
 Select “Industry Analysis”

 Select “Bank data and

statistics”
 Select “Institution directory”
 Scroll down to “Find
institutions”
 Form pops up
 Enter name of bank
 When list comes up, click Cert
Num to see details

 Another source: Uniform Bank

Performance Report
 Also accessible via the FDIC
site
 Provides comparative data over
time
 Also can provide “peer group”
comparisons
 We use SDI (Statistics on
Depository Institutions)
