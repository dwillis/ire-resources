5 great tools for journalists
in 50 minutes
By Matt Carroll, MIT Media Center <matt54@media.mit.edu;
@MattAtMIT>
If you are looking for an in-depth learning experience in any one tool
-- wrong place. This ain’t it. But if you want a quick overview of 5
newsroom-tested tools, you are in the right place.
-- Google Drive (Google Docs). A cloud-based solution to Windows
Office.
-- DocumentCloud. Analyze, annotate, and publish your documents.
-- Census Reporter. The way the US Census should have designed their
site. http://censusreporter.org/
-- NICAR listserv. Expert technical advice.
-- Google Fusion Tables. Create embeddable, linkable charts and
maps, with a medium learning curve
----------------------------1. Google Drive
Think “Office” on the Cloud. Create folders, text documents,
spreadsheets, presentations, and forms.
Requirements:
-- A gmail account

-- Learning curve: Each tool is different; range is from easy to
medium.
Tutorial: https://sites.google.com/site/gdocswebquest/
