What's an API?
“Application Programming Interface”
A way for one program to access another
 Examples:
 Request services from Windows or Mac OS
 Request, alter, submit content (“web service”)



Why Are They Valuable?
They make our content portable
 Web
 Phones/tablets
 Devices yet to be invented

They make “data rot” less of a problem
 Can always fetch latest data
 Less maintenance
Potential business benefits
 Partnerships, barter
 Expand the reach of your content
