Data Visualization (‘Viz’)?
Displaying numbers or text as charts,
graphs, diagrams, maps, etc.
Simple or complex, its power is translating
data visually at a glance.
Don’t be intimidated, or afraid to
experiment. We’re all learning.

Why ‘Viz’ Matters
It increases access to data for readers,
allowing them to discover what it means
Conveys data simply, but also adds
precision, transparency, clarity to
reporting
It’s also powerful.
