Investigating Breaking News
TIPSHEET
 Think creatively
 What ELSE do I want to know about this breaking news?
 What would help viewers/readers understand this event
more?
 What DON’T we know after the obvious “nuts and bolts”?
 What will help our organization’s coverage ‘stand out’
from our competition?
 What data might be available?
 How easily is it accessed?
 Is it in a form I can understand and analyze?
 How fast can I understand and analyze it?
 What value could it provide to our coverage?
 Where do I go?
 Find relationships, put event into context.
 Keep a special database folder to turn to when breaking
news hits; Use databases often
 Know how to turn data into interesting copy
 Make my pitch.
